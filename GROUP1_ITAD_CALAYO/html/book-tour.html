Online Booking

  1. Admission is free but ALL visitors are required to RESERVE ONLINE at least a day before the visit.
  2. Confirmation of booking will be sent through email.
  3. Group of reservations are limited to five (5) persons only.
  4. Walk-in visitors will NOT be accommodated.

Staying safe in the museums

  1. A temperature scan is required upon entry in each building.
  2. Persons with temperature of 37.5 and above will not be admitted.
  3. A Health Declaration FORM must be filled up before entering the museum. This may be done online through StaySafePh mobile app.
  4. Face masks must be worn at all times.
  5. Social distancing of at least 2 meters between persons not belonging to the same household must be observed at all times.
