All Out Peace Not War

 ›

The “All Out Peace Not War: Kalinaw Hindi Digmaan” is a traveling exhibition of large-scale paintings by Bert Monterona. It is currently at the National Museum Western-Southern Mindanao in Fort Pilar, Zamboanga City.

It features 22 works of Monterona highlighting his advocacies such as, campaigning against war, building a culture of peace, protecting the rights of women, promoting sustainable living, and safeguarding the environment. This also brings into focus the role of art in preserving indigenous and traditional creativity and custom, as well as addressing issues of social justice and community development, particularly in the Southern Philippines.
